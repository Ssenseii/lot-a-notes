DO's: 

	- use hueshifting
	- use antialysing
	- have a limited color palette
	- ease in out
	- pixel perfect lines and curves
	- dark colors
	- use contrast
	- shade with form
	- outline exterior when required
 	- use simple flat colors
	- use small canvas
	- 

DON'Ts: 

	- use the same hue
	- overuse Antialyzing
	- use every color in the book
	- use linear animations
	- jagged or doubles (misplaced pixels)
	- dont use black for outlines
	- use close together shades
 	- pillow or pancake shade (from dark to light... like topography)
	- outline all the times
	- use soft brushes and gradients
	- use large canvas
